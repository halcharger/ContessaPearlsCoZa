Contessa Pearls

Contessa Products began in the early 1950s in a little shop in the Salisbury Arcade in the city of Durban. Today, 60 years later Contessa is still thriving and now based in the suburbs in Westville, Durban. Contessa specialises in pearl and semi-precious stone jewellery as well as offering repairs and redesigning of existing jewellery. We offer our services to both the jewellery industry and direct to the public.
